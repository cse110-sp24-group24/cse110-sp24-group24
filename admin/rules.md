# 🤝 Team Working Agreement & Rules

> **Our Mission:** To build a software solution that addresses a real-world problem while making strong, well-reasoned engineering choices.

---

## Core Values & Guiding Principles
To ensure a healthy and productive environment, our team commits to upholding the following values:
* **Kindness & Respect:** Always be kind to one another and foster a supportive group dynamic.
* **Open Communication:** Keep channels open, transparent, and responsive.
* **Collaboration:** Bridge the gap between our different skill sets and work together as a unified front.
* **Efficient & Quality Work:** Maintain a high standard for our code and artifacts while respecting everyone's time.

## Team Goals & Outcomes
We are highly motivated to achieve the following milestones together:
- [ ] Cultivate great team chemistry and a positive working environment.
- [ ] Deliver a fully functional, high-quality software solution.
- [ ] Learn from one another and successfully navigate the software engineering process.

## Strengths & Unique Skills
Our team brings a diverse and powerful set of skills to the table:
* **Technical Versatility:** We have a strong mix of Front-end (HTML) and Back-end coding backgrounds.
* **Hackathon Experience:** Several members have proven abilities to build, collaborate, and ideate quickly under pressure.
* **Leadership:** Itai brings valuable project leadership and coordination skills from past hackathon experiences.

## Challenges & Mitigation Strategies
We recognize that there are levels to building software, and every team has areas for improvement. Here is how we will address our identified risks:

| Identified Challenge | How We Will Address It |
| :--- | :--- |
| **No prior experience writing Markdown** | We will experience a transformation together, learning the syntax as a group and helping each other format our documentation. |
| **Scheduling conflicts and limited meeting times** | We will prioritize proactive communication and heavily utilize asynchronous updates to keep the project moving. |

## Mutual Expectations
To succeed and reach our milestones, this is exactly what we require from one another:
* **Consistent Effort & Passion:** Bring your best energy and commitment to the project.
* **Schedule Transparency:** Openly discuss your individual availability and highlight any risks regarding time commitments or resource management early on.
* **A Safe Space:** Support one another so everyone feels comfortable sharing ideas, asking questions, and making mistakes.
