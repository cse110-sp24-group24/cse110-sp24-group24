## Discussion Points & Notes

**1. Project Assignment Review**
* The team reviewed their assigned project, which is to build a coding-themed typing game.

**2. Game Concept Brainstorming**
* **Duolingo/TypeRacer Hybrid:** Samuel suggested a game featuring positive reinforcement, satisfying animations, and potential bot interactions.
* **Tower Defense/Roguelite:** Janoj shared an idea inspired by existing typing games where enemies approach a central tower, and the player types words, HTML tags, or CSS identifiers to shoot them. Samuel noted this "roguelite" direction allows for easy implementation of upgrades and power-ups.
* **Boss Battles & Functions:** The team discussed having smaller enemies represent single lines of code, while bosses would require typing full methods or functions to defeat.
* **Boxing Concept:** Itai proposed a boxing game where the CPU hits you, and you must type a line of code within 10 seconds to recover from being knocked out.
* **Theme Shift:** The group agreed to abandon their previous "space retro" and slot machine themes in favor of exploring these new concepts.

**3. Meeting Schedule Setup**
