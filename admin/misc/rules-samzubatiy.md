# Personal Goals & Working Rules
*By Samuel Zubatiy*

> **My Objective:** To actively grow both as an engineer and as a teammate, ensuring my contributions leave a positive, lasting impact on our group dynamic.

## Leadership & Collaboration
* **Lead with Empathy and Impact:** I want to be the kind of leader my team remembers fondly. My goal is to guide the team effectively so that my peers feel supported, respected, and have a genuinely positive experience working with me.
* **Evolve as a Teammate:** I am highly motivated to bridge the gap between individual effort and team success. I want to learn how to collaborate seamlessly, adapting to the team's needs and lifting others up when things get difficult.

## Communication & Feedback
* **Spur Meaningful Dialogue:** I want to be a catalyst for good conversations. Instead of just passively agreeing, I aim to ask the right questions that help us navigate trade-offs and make strong engineering choices together.
* **Provide Constructive Feedback:** I will practice deep listening and offer feedback that is honest, helpful, and kind—ensuring it always focuses on improving our product and our development process without stepping on toes.

## Technical Growth
* **Master the Fundamentals:** I want to experience a real transformation in my foundational web skills. Specifically, I aim to vastly improve my understanding and execution of semantic **HTML**.
* **Documentation Excellence:** I want to become highly proficient at crafting clean, professional **Markdown** files so our team's documentation is always organized, readable, and perfectly formatted.
